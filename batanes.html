Basco Light House

is a stunning historic landmark that has become an emblem of the Batanes archipelago. Built in 1908, the lighthouse stands atop Naidi Hill, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and the Pacific Ocean. Visitors can climb to the top for an impressive vista of the rugged coastline, rolling hills, and nearby islands. The area is also known for its dramatic weather, making it a picturesque spot for photography, especially during sunrise and sunset. Surrounded by lush greenery and traditional Ivatan stone houses, Basco Lighthouse is not just a functional beacon but also a symbol of the rich maritime heritage of the region, attracting both tourists and locals seeking to experience the natural beauty and tranquility of Batanes.





































House of Dakay

an iconic Ivatan house in Batanes, is renowned for its stunning architecture and historical significance. Constructed from local lime and stone, it features the traditional design elements that reflect the resilience of the Ivatan people against harsh weather conditions. Named after Jose Dakay Estrella, the beloved nephew of owner Luisa Estrella, the house stands as a testament to family heritage and local culture. Its picturesque charm has made it one of the most photographed sites in the region, drawing visitors who admire its unique structure and the rich stories it holds. The House of Dakay not only represents architectural beauty but also embodies the warmth and hospitality of the Ivatan community.





































Fundacion Pacitain

is a remarkable initiative focused on preserving the rich cultural heritage and natural environment of the region. Founded by a group of dedicated individuals, the organization promotes sustainable tourism and supports local communities through various projects. The foundation offers programs that highlight traditional Ivatan practices, crafts, and cuisine, ensuring that these cultural treasures are passed down to future generations. Additionally, Fundacion Pacitain plays a vital role in environmental conservation efforts, protecting the unique landscapes and biodiversity of Batanes. Visitors can engage with local artisans and learn about the rich history and traditions of the Ivatan people, making it a meaningful stop for anyone exploring the islands.
