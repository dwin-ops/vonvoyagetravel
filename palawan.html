Puerto Prinsesa

The Capital City of Palawan in the Philippines, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty and rich biodiversity. Often referred to as the City in a Forest, it boasts lush landscapes and is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, which features one of the longest underground rivers in the world. The city offers a range of attractions, including beautiful beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and eco- tourism activities like island hopping, snorkeling, and diving. Puerto Princesa is also known for its commitment to conservation and sustainable tourism, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. With its unique blend of culture, history, and natural wonders, Puerto Princesa is a captivating destination in the Philippines.





































Coron

located in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines, is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty and stunning marine biodiversity. Famous for its crystal-clear lagoons, pristine beaches, and dramatic limestone cliffs, Coron is a paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers. The area is particularly well-known for its excellent diving spots, including WWII shipwrecks that attract divers from around the globe. Kayangan Lake, often hailed as one of the cleanest lakes in Asia, and Barracuda Lake are must-visit highlights. Coron is also a gateway to the stunning Calamian Islands, offering opportunities for island hopping, snorkeling, and enjoying the vibrant underwater life.
